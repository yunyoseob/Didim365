Table Name: Fruit

1. **FruitID**: Fruit ID
   - This is a unique identifier for each fruit.

2. **Name**: Fruit Name
   - Represents the name of the fruit.

3. **Color**: Color
   - Indicates the color of the fruit.

4. **Taste**: Taste
   - Describes the taste of the fruit.

5. **Price**: Price
   - Represents the price of the fruit.

6. **Season**: Season
   - Indicates the season in which the fruit is typically harvested.
