Table Name: Car

1. **CarID**: Car ID
   - This is a unique identifier for each car.

2. **Brand**: Brand
   - Represents the brand or manufacturer of the car.

3. **Model**: Model
   - Indicates the model name of the car.

4. **Year**: Year
   - Represents the manufacturing year of the car.

5. **Color**: Color
   - Indicates the color of the car.

6. **Price**: Price
   - Represents the price of the car.

7. **Mileage**: Mileage
   - Indicates the total mileage (distance traveled) of the car.

8. **Transmission**: Transmission
   - Indicates the type of transmission system used in the car (e.g., automatic, manual).
